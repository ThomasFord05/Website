Interior FAQ
Common Questions
What does your interior detailing include? +
Express includes vacuuming, surface wipe-down, interior glass cleaning, and console detailing. Premier adds carpet and upholstery shampooing, leather conditioning, full sanitization, odor treatment, stain removal, and pet hair removal.
How long does interior detailing take? +
The Express typically takes 1.5–2.5 hours. The Premier takes 2.5–4 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. We never rush — quality is always the priority.
How often should I get my interior detailed? +
We recommend an interior detail every 3–4 months. If you have pets, small children, or use your vehicle heavily, you may benefit from more frequent visits or our maintenance plan.
Do I need to prepare anything before you arrive? +
Please remove personal belongings from the vehicle before we arrive. This helps us work efficiently and ensures nothing gets misplaced. If the vehicle is heavily cluttered, additional charges may apply.

Exterior FAQ
Common Questions
What is 1-step paint correction? +
A 1-step paint correction uses a machine polisher with a single compound or polish to remove light swirl marks, surface scratches, and oxidation. It significantly improves gloss and clarity without the time and cost of a full multi-stage correction.
How often should I get an exterior detail? +
We recommend an exterior detail every 2–3 months to maintain the protective layer and keep your vehicle looking its best. Our maintenance plan makes this easy and cost-effective.
Will detailing remove all scratches? +
The Paint Enhancement with 1-step correction removes light surface scratches and swirl marks effectively. Deep scratches that penetrate through the clear coat may require more advanced treatment — contact us for a free assessment.
How long does exterior detailing take? +
The standard Exterior Detail takes approximately 1.5–2.5 hours. The Paint Enhancement with paint correction typically takes 3–5 hours depending on vehicle size and paint condition.

Full Detail FAQ
Common Questions
Which package should I pick? +
The Express is best for regular upkeep and budget-conscious clients. The Premier is our most popular — ideal for families and daily drivers wanting a thorough clean without paint correction. The Signature is for those who want the absolute best, including paint correction and premium protection.
How long does a full detail take? +
Express typically takes 2–3 hours. Premier takes 3–5 hours. The Signature can take 5–7 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. We never rush — we deliver quality every time.
How often should I get a full detail? +
We recommend a full detail every 3–6 months depending on your driving habits and lifestyle. Our maintenance plan is a great way to keep your vehicle in top shape year-round at a consistent cadence.
Can detailing improve my vehicle's resale value? +
Absolutely. Regular full detailing protects your paint, prevents wear and tear on interior surfaces, and keeps your vehicle in top condition — all of which significantly boost resale value when the time comes to sell.
